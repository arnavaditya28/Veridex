Assess Public Information Reliably

A transparent platform that evaluates the reliability of widely shared public claims using rule-based indicators, assisted analysis, and community discussion — without declaring absolute truth.

Public Claims

Reliability indicators for commonly shared public information

How Veridex Works

1. Claim Submission

Users submit publicly circulating information or claims without revealing their identity. Claims remain neutral at the time of submission.

2. Source Attachment

Supporting sources such as official notices, media reports, or public references can be attached to provide context and evidence.

3. Rule-Based Evaluation

The system applies predefined, deterministic rules based on source type, count, freshness, and consistency to generate reliability indicators.

4. AI-Assisted Analysis (Advisory)

Artificial intelligence is used only to structure claims, detect similarities, highlight conflicts, and summarize information. AI does not determine factual correctness or override system rules.

Why Veridex Matters

In an age of rapid information sharing, misinformation often spreads faster than verification. Veridex provides a structured and transparent mechanism to assess reliability while preserving user autonomy and interpretive freedom.

Transparency

Reliability indicators are derived from explainable rules and visible signals rather than opaque or black-box decisions.

Public-Centric

Designed for students, citizens, and organizations dealing with widely shared public information.

Ethically Safe

The platform avoids automated truth judgments and does not suppress discussion or dissenting viewpoints.

Core System Principles

  • ✔ Reliability is not equivalent to absolute truth
  • ✔ Evaluations are deterministic and rule-based
  • ✔ Community discussion does not alter reliability indicators
  • ✔ Artificial intelligence assists analysis but does not decide outcomes
  • ✔ All system behavior remains transparent and auditable

AI Usage Disclaimer

Any AI-generated insights on Veridex are intended solely to assist users by organizing information, detecting patterns, and highlighting uncertainty. The platform does not rely on artificial intelligence to declare information as true or false.
