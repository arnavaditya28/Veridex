About Veridex

Transparency through structured discussion and rule-based reliability evaluation

🔍 What is Veridex?

Veridex is a claim verification and community discussion platform that enables users to critically evaluate publicly circulating information. Each claim has a dedicated discussion space and a transparent, rule-based reliability score — no black-box decisions.

⚠️ Problem Statement

Misinformation spreads across digital platforms without accountability or context. Citizens and organizations lack structured tools to evaluate claims objectively, distinguish reliable sources from unreliable ones, or trace the origin of widely shared information.

💡 Our Approach

  • Claim-specific discussion boards for structured debate
  • Multiple content formats — text, links, images, polls
  • Authenticated participation with Trust Scores
  • Community voting, replies, and moderation
  • Deterministic reliability indicators — no AI decides truth
