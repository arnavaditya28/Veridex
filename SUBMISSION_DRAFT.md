## One-line pitch
A public information reliability platform where users submit claims, attach supporting sources, and the community evaluates and discusses how trustworthy they are.

## Problem
Online claims spread faster than they can be verified, and there's rarely a structured, transparent way for a community to weigh the evidence behind a claim.

## Solution
Veridex gives each claim a dedicated space to gather sources (including parsed PDF evidence), be evaluated for reliability, and host an open discussion — with moderation to keep it trustworthy.

## Key Features
- Claim submission and community evaluation
- Source attachments with automatic PDF parsing
- Threaded discussions per claim
- JWT authentication with email verification
- Moderator role and tools
- Security-hardened backend (Helmet, rate limiting, sanitization, XSS protection)

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB (Mongoose), JWT, bcryptjs, Multer, Nodemailer
